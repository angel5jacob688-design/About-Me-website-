About Me

Hey, I’m Angel Jacob — a student from Kerala who finds wonder in the little things. I’m that girl who pauses to admire the evening sky brushed with pink, or the quiet sparkle of a star-filled night. I love seeing beauty in simple moments — though it sometimes leaves me lost in thought. I enjoy exploring new possibilities and finding the “why” behind what I do. That sense of purpose keeps me grounded and inspired.

My Creative Side

I’ve always been drawn to art — I love sketching people, objects, and abstract ideas that express emotion. To me, art isn’t just what we see on a canvas; it’s how we feel when we look at the world differently. Designing spaces and layouts — especially interior design — is something I quietly nurture as a side passion.

My Journey

Right now, I’m pursuing my engineering degree while exploring the world of coding. I’m still at the beginning, but I love how programming combines creativity and logic. Developing my first web page feels like a small but meaningful step toward blending both — a step that excites me to keep learning and creating.

My Interests

My interests are a mix of imagination, empathy, and exploration. I love connecting ideas, people, and creativity in ways that help me grow.

  • Drawing people, objects, and abstract ideas
  • Designing interiors and visual layouts
  • Reading and writing poetry and literature
  • Learning about psychology and different perspectives
  • Traveling and experiencing cultures and cuisines
  • Enjoying rainy evenings, deep talks, and quiet walks
  • Building web design and public speaking skills
  • Strengthening teamwork, communication, and leadership

Each of these passions adds to who I’m becoming — creative, thoughtful, and eager to keep evolving.

Looking Ahead

I’m excited to see how I’ll grow into my future self — how the choices and habits I’m building now will shape who I become. Every small step feels like part of something larger I’m working toward.
